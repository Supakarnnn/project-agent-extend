# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Back-office administrators managing the product catalog and keeping Vera AI's recommendation data current.

## Product Purpose

Provide one reliable workflow for catalog management in Supabase and intentional synchronization to Vera AI through Milvus embeddings.

## Brand Personality

Premium, clinical, calm. Confident and concise without feeling cold.

## Anti-references

Generic SaaS dashboards, decorative glass effects, noisy gradients, playful consumer UI, and unfamiliar controls for standard admin tasks.

## Design Principles

- Make system state unmistakable.
- Keep destructive or expensive actions intentional.
- Preserve familiar admin workflows.
- Use motion only to communicate progress and completion.
- Keep Vera synchronization separate from catalog persistence.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Maintain keyboard access, visible focus, readable contrast, semantic dialogs, and reduced-motion behavior.
